1. Information collected

We collect information in order to provide better services to all users. We have two ways to collect information:

  • You provide us with information. When you register your ABOJ account, we will ask you to provide personal information, such as your email, real name, etc.
    You can also spontaneously provide us with this information on the way to use the service later
  • We collect information when you use the service We will collect information about the services you use and how you use them, such as when you used our services

2. How to use the collected information

We use the information collected from all services to provide, maintain, protect and improve these services, while developing new services and protecting the privacy of ABOJ and users
When we want to use the information for other purposes not specified in this privacy policy, we will ask for your consent in advance.

3. Access and update your personal information

No matter when you use our service, we strive to enable you to access your personal information. If the information is incorrect, we will try to let you quickly update or delete the information in various ways. When updating your personal information, we may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request
We strive to maintain our services perfectly to protect information from accidental or malicious damage. Therefore, when you delete information from our service, we may not immediately delete the residual copies of these information from the server in use, or we may not delete the corresponding information from the backup system.

4. Information security

We provide you with SSL encrypted access services. You can use our services through SSL (https), which can largely ensure the security of your information during transmission
For sensitive data (such as your password), we promise to perform high-intensity irreversible encryption during storage. The administrators and maintenance personnel of ABOJ can not access the clear text of sensitive data, and all operations are free of manual intervention.
